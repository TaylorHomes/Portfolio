Why social media archiving is important

Social media archiving protects your agency and helps inform your citizens.

Archiving ensures compliance with public records laws and social media retention requirements. Agencies need to be prepared to respond to public record requests. In all 50 states social media is considered public record, and agencies have an obligation to comply with open records laws. Social media archiving is the only way to consistently respond to records requests.
